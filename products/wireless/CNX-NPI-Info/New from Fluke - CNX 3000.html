The Fluke CNX Wireless System is here! »

Fluke has once again revolutionized the way work is done in industrial, electrical and HVAC environments.

Introducing the Fluke CNX Wireless System: a suite of five tools that connect together wirelessly to make your job easier.

The Fluke CNX Wireless System delivers real-time measurements that are remote, simultaneous, and recordable. The CNX Wireless Multimeter serves as the hub for the system and takes readings from four types of wireless modules: AC current clamp, iFlex current clamp, AC voltage, and temperature. All at the same time. All on the same screen. All from 20 meters away.

Send live or logged measurements to a computer with the PC adapter. It's that simple.
