Fluke Calibration Announces:
5502A Multi-Product Calibrator
now available

Introducing the new 5502A Multi-Product Calibrator, with the best range of functions, accuracy and value in its class.

The new 5502A covers a wide workload, including 3.5 and 4.5 digit multimeters, clamp meters, thermometers, and much more. Its versatile functions include:

  • Voltage to 1020 V, dc or ac
  • Current to 20.5 A, dc or ac
  • Fully variable resistance to 1100 MΩ
  • AC/DC power with variable phase
  • Continuously variable capacitance to 110 mF
  • More than 18 temperature calibration functions for thermocouples or RTDs
  • Options to calibrate oscilloscopes to 600 MHz
  • Current to 120 A when paired with the new 52120A Transconductance Amplifier
Precision. Performance. Confidence.
