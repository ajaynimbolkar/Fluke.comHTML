  Fluke Calibration Announces:
 

The 6135A/PMUCAL Phasor Measurement Unit Calibration System

Calibrate PMUs with speed and confidence

Verify a single Phasor Measurement Unit configuration (nominal frequency, sample rate and class) in hours rather than weeks.

The 6135A/PMUCAL Phasor Measurement Unit Calibration System lets you:

  • Perform the static and dynamic tests prescribed by the latest IEEE Std C37.118.1™-2011, IEEE Standard for Synchrophasor Measurements for Power Systems.
  • Complete the 500 tests that certify a PMU configuration in less than one day using an automated system, versus two to six weeks with manual test methods.
  • Establish traceability to national standards for the calibrations of your PMU assets.
