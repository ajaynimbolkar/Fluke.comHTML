The 2638A Hydra Series III
Data Acquisition System/Digital Multimeter

Discover a breakthrough in
stand-alone data acquisition.

Precision dc measurement and thermocouple accuracy, full color display, easy-to-use menu system and CAT II industrial safety ratings put the 2638A in a category of its own as a precision data acquisition system.

The dedicated 6.5 digit digital multimeter mode provides additional utility and value.

2638A Hydra Series III features at a glance:

  • DC accuracy of 0.0024 %
  • Thermocouple accuracy up to 0.36 °C
  • Up to 66 universal differential isolated inputs
  • 300V CAT II input safety reading
  • On-screen color trend graphing
  • Easy-to-use menu system for setup and data management
