  Fluke Calibration Announces:
 

The 5730A Multifunction Calibrator

Introducing the 5730A: the next generation of the world's most accurate multifunction electrical calibrator.

For 25 years, you've trusted the reliability, accuracy and ease of use of Fluke 5700 Series calibrators. Now the Fluke Calibration 5730A Multifunction Calibrator raises that standard of performance for a new generation. Features include:

  • Improved accuracy that reduces the need for guardbanding and increases test confidence
  • A dynamic new color touch screen that makes it quick and easy to operate, navigate and control, thus reducing setup and calibration times
  • New Visual Connection Management™ terminals that light up to help with cable configurations and protect both the user and instrument
Find out how easily the new 5730A can help you improve your lab's performance. Get complete product details and request a quote at www.flukecal.eu/5730A.
