Fluke Calibration Announces:
52120A Transconductance Amplifier
now available

The 52120A Transconductance Amplifier lets you test and calibrate a broad workload at full current range.

The 52120A expands your calibration capabilities to power and energy meters, clamp meters, CTs and Rogowski coils – up to 6000 Amps. Use it with all Fluke Calibration calibrators, including the 5080A, 9100, 55XX and 57XX Series. For enhanced accuracy, use it closed-loop with the 610X Electrical Power Standards.

  • Output ranges: 2 A, 20 A, 120 A
  • Frequency to 10 kHz
  • Generate 3,000 or 6,000 amps with accessory coils
  • Industry-leading amplifier accuracy
