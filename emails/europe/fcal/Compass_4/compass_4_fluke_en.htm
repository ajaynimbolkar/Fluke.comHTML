COMPASS for Pressure V4.0
Product Update

Support for Ruska piston gauges, seat-based licensing, and more...

COMPASS for Pressure Software automates calibration of almost any pressure calibration device, from piston gauges to transfer standards.

Version 4.0 includes fully integrated support for Ruska auto-float controllers and piston gauges, plus auto-detect support for Ruska references. And now you can export COMPASS data directly to a MET/BASE database, enabling MET/TRACK software to manage all of your assets.

Version 4.0 also features seat-based licensing so users can expand from a single user to multiple users quickly and economically.
