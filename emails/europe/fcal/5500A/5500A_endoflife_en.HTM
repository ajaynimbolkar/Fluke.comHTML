Last Time Buy announcement for the Fluke 5500A Multiproduct Calibrator

Now is the time to buy one of the last 5500A's.

Fluke’s 5500A, the world’s first multi-product calibrator, gained a solid reputation as a real workhorse as soon as it was introduced. It’s known worldwide for its ability to calibrate a wide range of electrical test instruments at an affordable price.

Fluke Calibration announces that the production of these units will be stopped and that they still have a limited number of units available.

Especially for 5500A users with Met/Cal software and procedures written around the 5500A, now is the time to buy one of the last 5500A's available. All units will have their normal warranty and come with an accredited report of calibration. Full service and repair of these calibrators will continue for at least five years after production ceases.

Orders will be accepted on a first come basis as long as stock lasts.
