RF calibration just became less complicated 

Introducing the Fluke Calibration 96270A RF Reference Source:
It's the simplest, most accurate, and cost-effective way to calibrate spectrum analyzers, RF power sensors and more, to 27 GHz.

Now you can calibrate spectrum analyzers, power sensors, modulation meters, frequency counters, high-frequency oscilloscopes and much more, with a single instrument. The 96270A covers more than 80% of the test points required for calibrating almost all spectrum analyzers of any frequency range. For many spectrum analyzers operating below 27 GHz, you only need a 96270A to perform the entire calibration. You can't simplify much more than that!

The 96270A features "what you set is what you get" accuracy and signal purity, helping you to avoid losses, mismatch errors, and uncertainty contributions from cables, other devices and interconnections. Designed specifically for RF calibration, the 96270A's graphical interface is easy to learn and to operate.

With the 96270A, you have fewer instruments to purchase and fewer to maintain, resulting in major cost savings. In fact, you can cut your RF calibration costs in half or even more.

Learn more about how the 96270A RF Reference Source can simplify RF calibration in your laboratory: www.flukecal.eu/96270A.
