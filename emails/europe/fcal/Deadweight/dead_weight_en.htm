  Fluke Calibration Deadweight Testers

Accurate, robust and flexible pressure measurement standards 

Fluke Calibration offers a full range of stable, rugged and economical pressure standards. Models are offered to calibrate devices from vacuum and low pneumatic pressures to very high hydraulic pressures. These include P3000 pneumatic deadweight testers and the P3100, P3200 and P3800 hydraulic deadweight testers. The 6531/6532 E-DWT Electronic Deadweight Testers are a digital alternative to the conventional models.

P3000 Series Pneumatic Deadweight Testers
These deadweight testers can be used to calibrate virtually any pressure sensing device, including transducers, transmitters, gauges or pressure switches.

  • Pressure ranges from vacuum through 2,000 psi (140 Bar)
  • Accuracy better than 0.015 % of reading.
  • Gas operated with optional hand pumps

6531/6532 E-DWT Electronic Deadweight Tester Kits
A lighter, easier-to-use deadweight tester calibration alternative that is at home in the metrology and calibration labs, on the production floor or in the field.

  • Pressure ranges available to 2000 bar (30,000 psi)
  • ± 0.02% of reading total one-year year accuracy
  • Compatible with wide range of liquids, including water
