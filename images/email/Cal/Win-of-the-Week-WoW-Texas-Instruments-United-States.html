How FCAL Is Making An Impact? This facility performs long-term hi-pot testing of electrical components. This testing involves applying 10kv to the components at up to 150˚C. The existing process had been developed in-house but the equipment did not provide the stability or control that their evolving testing needs required for their latest generation power ICs and devices.

TI approached the FCAL custom-bath team to develop a suitable system: Given the specifics of the testing and the precautions needed when testing at these elevated temperatures and voltages, TI felt that this system required a level of knowledge and expertise that was beyond their in-house design and manufacturing capabilities. They asked us to provide a bath with racks, component handling and specially designed lid to manage the high-voltage cabling.
How/Reason We Won?
We won by offering a breadth of product, knowledge, expertise and support that is un-rivaled in the industry. The American Fork based customs team was able to fully understand the customer’s needs, using their experience to the extent that they were able to advise on potential issues and needs that the customer had not considered, and so designing and developing a solution that both meets their current needs and also can evolve as the customer’s needs evolve. Developing a solution for this level of application required an on-site consultation in addition to the engineering work performed in the factory. The Fluke Calibration’s RPM was able to provide both the on-site engineering consultation and also the sales skills necessary to close the order.

The customer is now adding capacity locally by acquiring a second bath and additional racks for part handling. They are planning to acquire further baths for sister facilities in other locations.
