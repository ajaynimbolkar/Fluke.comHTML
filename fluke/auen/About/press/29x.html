Fluke Australia releases new 29x series of universal waveform generators

Sydney, 9 June 2006: Fluke Australia today announced the launch of its new 29x series universal waveform generators, boosting Fluke’s complete range of signal source generators.

 

Designed for low frequency audio manufacturing and service applications, the waveform generators produce multiple signals to test complex electronic circuits. The waveform generators define, simulate and store custom complex waveforms to pinpoint faults in a device.

 

The series includes the Model 291 single channel, Model 292 two channel, and Model 294 four channel generators. 29x series features include:

 

·        1MB point waveform memory

·        Unlimited storage using a removable compact flash memory card

·        Variable clock sampling for full-featured programmability

·        Accurate arbitrary waveform generation

·        Waveform Manager Plus software (creates, manipulates and manages arbitrary waveforms within a single Windowsâ-based program).

 

“The Fluke 29x series adds to Fluke’s high-resolution, fast generating test and measurement tools. The waveform generators have been designed specifically for complex circuits providing high performance and reliability at highly competitive prices”, said Dennis Juniper, Precision Measurement Channel Manager, Fluke Australia.
