Fluke 700G Precision Test Gauges
The new 700G Precision Pressure Test Gauge offers 9 models with measurements from 15 psi/1bar to 10,000 psi/690 bar. And best-in-class 0.05% accuracy. It combines ease of use with legendary Fluke durability.

When used with 700G/Track Software, these precision test gauges are able to remotely log nearly 8,500 pressure measurements in real time to a PC. Measurements can also be uploaded and displayed or exported for further analysis

What's more, the 700G comes with optional hydraulic or pneumatic test pressure kits, complete with pumps, hoses, adapters and hard case. In short, it's the complete pressure measurement solution in a convenient carrying case.

Fluke 717/718 Pressure Calibrators
Fluke has also upgraded its 717/718 Pressure Calibrators with increased accuracy of .025%. The 717 also has two new pressure ranges, one lower and one upper. Both units are about 1/3 the size of comparable tools, and are much easier to use than other pressure calibrators
