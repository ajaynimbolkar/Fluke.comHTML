Be the best in class with the Fluke Student Kit

The new Fluke Electricians starter Kit consists of the key basic test tools for new and trainee Electricians. The kit contains the Fluke T90 Two Pole Electrical Tester, the Fluke SM100 Socket Tester and the Fluke 1AC Non Contact Voltage Detector. The kit is supplied with the rugged Fluke C1600 ToolBox which can be used to carry these and other tools to the job.

 

Fluke T90 Electrical Tester

  • Fast test results the way you need them, with large, easy-to-use buttons, bright backlights, and clear indicators

Fluke 1AC-II VoltAlert™

  • Voltbeat™ technology and continuous self-test — so you always know it’s working.

Fluke SM100 Socket Tester

  • Clear LED indication of wiring status.

Fluke C1600 Toolbox

  • Deep interior large enough to hold and protect your gear.
