Fluke 376 FC True-rms AC/DC Clamp Meter with iFlex®

The Fluke 376 FC Wireless True-rms AC/DC Clamp Meter offers logging capabilities, includes the iFlex™ Flexible Current Probe, and measures up to 1000 V and 1000 A, ac or dc.

  • iFlex flexible current probe expands the measurement range to 2500 A ac; provides access to large conductors in tight spaces (included)
  • TPAK magnetic hanging strap (included)
  • Integrated VFD low pass filter for accurate motor drive measurements
  • Proprietary inrush measurement technology to filter out noise and capture motor starting current exactly as the circuit protection sees it
  • CAT IV 600 V, CAT III 1000 V safety rating
  • Three-year warranty
  • Soft carrying case
