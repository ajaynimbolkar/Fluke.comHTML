How to understand and influence energy consumption?

With energy costs already high, and getting higher, many facilities are trying to reduce their energy consumption. Most have not previously analyzed their monthly energy usage, traced it to operational components, or broken out the utility fees. Until you understand how you’re using energy, it’s hard to make smart decisions on how to reduce consumption.
