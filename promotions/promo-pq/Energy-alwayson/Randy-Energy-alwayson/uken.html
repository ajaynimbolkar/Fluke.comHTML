It looks like you’re having a power quality issue

A computer locks up. Lights flicker. A motor overheats. These, and many other symptoms, can present themselves in any facility. They’re often considered minor or even normal— assumed to be the result of normal wear, minor bugs, or equipment aging. Too often the real cause is poor power quality. These problems can lead to downtime, excess in energy consumption, data loss, product damage and additional maintenance expenses.
