The power of Fluke Connect

View your energy quality data on your smartphone, tablet or desktop through the Fluke Condition Monitoring software solution. You’ll be able to track measurement fluctuations over days or months, collect trend data and graph analytics you can share with your team, and keep tabs on critical equipment from anywhere, at any time.

Smarter and safer

Taking readings on live circuits can be dangerous, and trying to pinpoint intermittent issues or identify trends means you’re spending a lot of time in the arc flash zone. With the 3540 FC, you simply set it up, define your parameters, and monitor equipment from a distance safely outside the arc flash zone, even miles away.

Data logging made easy

Characterize power quality, conduct load studies and capture hard-to-find voltage events over a defined period of time. Easily monetize the cost of energy waste due to poor power quality and take informed steps to make your facility as energy-efficient as possible.
