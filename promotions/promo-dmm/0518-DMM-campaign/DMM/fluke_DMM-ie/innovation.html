Multimeter innovations

Digital multimeters have been around since 1955. Yet Fluke innovations continue to advance the capabilities of these essential tools. The Fluke 233, for example, was the first ever digital multimeter to come with a detachable screen for making safer measurements at a distance. In 2012, Fluke introduced the CNX 3000 Series Wireless System, allowing for simultaneous readings of up to 10 remote modules.
