Reliable and repeatable results you can trust

Vibration testing has never been easier

Our innovative sensor design minimizes measurement variations caused by device angle or varying contact pressure. After just two seconds, you get a four-level severity scale of both bearing- and machine condition so you can plan your maintenance accordingly.

It’s a 3 in 1 tool: a must have for your tool belt: it checks bearings, overall vibration and temperature in one measurement.

Fluke Connect™ lets you share, export and trend machine condition data, and save time in reporting and troubleshooting – without leaving the field.

Take the confusion out of bearing assessment

Unique to the Fluke 805FC Vibration Meter is the Crest Factor Plus (CF+) technology. This provides reliable bearing assessment without the hassle of having to enter shaft dimensions and rotational speed. The bearing health is judged easily and within just a few seconds.

See what CF+ can do for you

Find where the fault lies – at a glance

The 805 has a built-in infrared thermometer to detect over-temperature at the point you’re measuring. That tells you if there’s a problem with your motor or bearings. Scanning problem motors and drives with our Ti400 IR camera will tell you at a glance exactly where the problem lies.
